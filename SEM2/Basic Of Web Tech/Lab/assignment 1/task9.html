ProBook X1500 Features

  • Display: 15.6-inch OLED display with 4K resolution (3840 x 2160) and HDR support.
  • Processor: Intel Core i9-13900K processor.
  • Memory: 32GB DDR5 RAM (expandable to 64GB).
  • Storage: 1TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 SSD.
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 Laptop GPU with 8GB GDDR6 VRAM.
  • Operating System: Windows 11 Pro.
  • Keyboard: Backlit keyboard with customizable RGB lighting and a dedicated number pad.
  • Touchpad: Precision touchpad with multi-gesture support.
  • Connectivity:
    • Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax)
    • Bluetooth 5.3
    • 2 x Thunderbolt 4 ports
    • 2 x USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-A ports
    • 1 x HDMI 2.1 port
    • 1 x SD card reader
    • 1 x 3.5mm headphone/microphone combo jack
  • Audio: Stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos support.
  • Webcam: 1080p FHD webcam with facial recognition.
  • Battery: 99Wh battery with fast charging support.
  • Design: Slim and lightweight aluminum chassis.
  • Security: Fingerprint reader and TPM 2.0 security chip.
  • Cooling: Advanced vapor chamber cooling system.
